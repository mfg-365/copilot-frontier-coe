What the Frontier CoE is: a strategic, value-led mechanism that connects efforts across the five drivers of AI value as AI scales. It doesn't centralize all AI work or replace existing teams — it orchestrates the interplay of strategy, data, technology, workforce, and governance to create shared visibility, prioritization, and guardrails. The result turns AI readiness into repeatable, enterprise-level business impact.

The human engine

Microsoft 365 Copilot Champion Teams

Champions are the influencers, power users, and advocates who accelerate adoption, reinforce new behaviors, and help scale what's working. They aren't the most technical people — they're the most trusted people in their team.

Why 2–3 champions per line of business

1

Context beats broadcast

A finance champion speaks finance; an HR champion speaks HR. Peer-proximate help in the language of the work drives adoption far better than central messaging.

2

Resilience, not a single point of failure

One champion goes on leave or gets busy and momentum dies. Two to three create redundancy so a team's network never goes dark.

3

Critical mass without dilution

A pair or trio shares the load and bounces ideas. Go to one and it's fragile; go to ten and ownership blurs and energy scatters.

Rule of thumb: scale the count with team size — roughly one champion per 20–40 employees, never fewer than two per line of business.

What a champion actually does

💬 Run office hours & demos

Host regular drop-in sessions and quick demos for their team.

📢 Share prompts & wins

Curate role-specific prompts and broadcast local success stories.

💡 Surface use cases

Spot high-value workflows and feed them to the CoE backlog.

📝 Give product feedback

Channel real user friction and feature requests back to Microsoft.

🤝 Model new behaviors

Visibly use Copilot daily so peers see what good looks like.

📈 Reinforce habits

Nudge, encourage, and keep momentum alive between launches.

Run the network with a rhythm

Peer channel

An always-on Teams channel for questions, prompts, and wins.

Monthly community call

Share updates, demos, and what's new across the program.

Prompt-a-thons

Hands-on events where teams discover real use cases together.

Recognition

Badges, shout-outs, and leaderboards make the role a point of pride.

Ongoing skilling

Curated paths plus the AI Skills Navigator keep skills current.

Feedback loop

A clear path to capture friction and route ideas to the CoE.
